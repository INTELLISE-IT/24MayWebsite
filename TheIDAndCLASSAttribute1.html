Cities of Maharashtra

Mumbai

Mumbai is the economic capital of India. It is the Capital of Maharashtra State. Mumbai is famous for many things, such as financial capital and business capital of India, film industry capital.

Solapur

Solapur is the district that is located on the border of Maharashtra State. The district is connected with the Karnataka, Andhra Pradesh border. Solapur is famous for textile industries.

Pune

Pune is the district that is located in the mid of Maharashtra State. The district is known for education. Pune is famous for IT industries.
